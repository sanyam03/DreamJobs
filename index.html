The course has limited seats, so please take some time to fill out your application and answer the questions to your best ability. There might be an interview call with us to assess if youre ready for the program.

The curriculum has been designed to allow people to participate part-time. However you must be able to dedicate a minimum of 20-30 hours per week based on the work needed to learn.

The course requires you to join the live classes, work on assignments, do mock interviews with your mentors. So no, the course cant be done at your own pace.

You are encouraged to join the sessions live to keep up with the curriculum and participate in discussions. If youre unable to attend, class recordings are not available for every class.

Yes. You only pay if you get placed from your salary.

Yes, you will receive a certificate on completion of the course. However, the skills you build are what will unlock opportunities for you, not the certificate.
